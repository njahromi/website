The Amazon Story

From Garage to Glamour.

          The success of Amazon is thanks to its ambitious founder and CEO, Jeff Bezos. Jeff Bezos started Amazon in 1994 as a web-based mail order service out of his Texas garage. As demand grew and surpassed supply, Jeff expanded the outreach of his business , purchasing a warehouse and branching out. As a company, Amazon has always valued frugality. The iconic "door desks" (Cheaply made desk consiting of an unfinished door nailed to a set of 4x4s) can still be seen in any amazon fulfillment center managagement office around the country. Amazon always knew that it is best to invest in employees and benefits rather than material goods, which is how it is able to gather and retain talent on a global scale. In addition to its mail order service, Amazon is a pioneer in cloud computing, having launched the Amazon Web Services program in the early 2000s. Amazon Web Services is widely acknowledged as the most secure and robust cloud computing infrastructure, and is trusted by the Federal Government for its impeccable data security. Each year, Amazon continues to grow and expand as brick and mortar retail stores diminish. Amazon shows consistent growth annually, regardless of how the economy is doing as a whole.
