Amazon opens 2.4 million-square-foot warehouse in Orlando

September 25th 2018

          Amazon has opened a new warehouse in Orlando Florida. Amazon has launched the project without seeking the aid of any local tax incentives, and spans a massive area of 128 acres, which will add to the already enormous and growing outreach central Florida. The company is set to add 1,500 new full and part-time jobs to the Orlando area, paying each associate at least $15.00 hourly regardless of rank.










Amazon Announces Free Holiday Shipping For All

November 3rd 2018

          In order to compete with Target, which recently announced free two-day shipping for all customers, Amazon has launched a free 5-8 shipping program for all customers. The program officially launched on Monday, November 5th, 2018. Amazon has described this program as "limited" siding with target and Best Buy who are also offering limited time free shipping.







Amazon Launches New Brick & Mortar Location

November 6th 2018

          Amazon has opened its newest 4-Star store location in Berkeley, California this weekend, adding to its growing number of Brick and Mortar locations. The location hosts Amazon's highest rated, new & trending products such as toys, games, and echoes.
